WebAudio Virtual Speakers System (WVS)

Using headphones or earphones, you feel the sound comes from just close to your ears or from the center of the head.
It's quite different from the sound in the audio room with speakers or the real concert hall.
WVS is a WebApp converts your favorite music to Binaural 3D sound and plays in natural audio space with headphones or earphones,
but is not so effective for the sound files mixed of separately recorded tracks in the studio or for playing by the real speaker systems.

Supported audio codec is FLAC, WAV, MP3, OGG, and requires a PC/Mobile Phones,Tablet supported WebAudio.
Use sliders / drag / swipe to move speakers.  ( x  : distance between the speakers  y/z: height / depth of the speakers. )
